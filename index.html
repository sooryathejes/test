SPECIAL KERALA FESTIVAL TOURS

DIWALI

Diwali, also known as Deepavali, is a joyous Hindu festival celebrated as the "Festival of Lights" to symbolize the triumph of light over darkness and good over evil.

THEYYAM

Theyyam is a vibrant and ancient ritual art form from Kerala, India, where performers adorned in elaborate costumes and intricate makeup embody divine spirits and deities.

KATHAKALI

Kathakali, a classical dance-drama from Kerala, India, combines intricate facial expressions, elaborate costumes, and dynamic movements to depict captivating stories from Hindu epics.
